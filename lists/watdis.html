Unequal Distribution

  • West Bank: Israeli settlers consume 300 litres per capita/day; Palestinians average 80 litres (WHO minimum is 100 litres).
  • Gaza: 90% of water from aquifer is unfit for drinking; 80% of Gaza population depends on contaminated water.
  • Mekorot (Israeli national water company) supplies 80% of West Bank water to settlements, while restricting Palestinian access.

International Law Violations

  • Geneva Convention IV, Article 53: Prohibits destruction of water infrastructure in occupied territory.
  • Amnesty International 2019: 'Israel's water apartheid denies Palestinians equitable access.'
  • Human Rights Watch 2021: 'Discriminatory water allocation is a key element of apartheid.'

Humanitarian Crisis

80% of Palestinian agricultural land in Jordan Valley is denied irrigation water, destroying livelihoods.
  • World Bank 2022: Water sector losses exceed $1.1 billion annually due to Israeli restrictions.
  • Palestinian communities in Area C: 180,000 people not connected to water network.
